nouns

A noun represents a person, place, animal, thing or an idea.

KINDS OF NOUN

Nouns are classified as follows:


Proper: It is the name of a particular object, whether person, place, animal, idea or thing.

Commmon : Is is the name possessed by things in general.

Gollective: It is the name representing a group of things

Abstract: It is the name representing qua lities, emotions, conditions or actions.

PRONOUNS

A pronoun is a word used in place of a noun. A pronoun is generally used to avoid repetition a noun.

KINDS OF NOUN

Pronouns are classified as follows:


Personal pronouns: It reders to a specific noun and used as a substitute of the noun.

Interrogative pronouns: It stands as the question about a person, place or a thing.

Indefinite pronouns: Is is used to refer to a person, thing or a place that are unknown.

Relative pronouns: It relares to a nouns, pronouns, word or expression preceding it in the sentence, and at the same time connects two statements.

Demonstrative pronouns: A noun or pronoun is specified and identified by it.

Reflexive pronouns: It usually follows a noun or a pronoun and used to emphasize the subject.
